Hello!
I am a 1st-year Ph.D. student in the Natural Language Processing group at the University of Washington. I am fortunate to be advised by Hannaneh Hajishirzi and Luke Zettlemoyer.

My primary research interests lie in the area of natural language processing and machine learning. In particular, I am interested in developing methods for various natural language understanding and question anwering tasks with limited supervision.
